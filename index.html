Sketches2014

An artistic exercise: following prompts from an artistic group, I develop generative art sketches approximately daily. Some sketches take me longer to complete. The prompts are coming from a group of people who are actually sketching things (not with code), so some may seem a little odd.

Since these are intended as purely creative, rough sketches, code quality may vary. My objective is to implement a visual idea, as quickly as I can.

What is a sketch? I see it as quick code...like sketching with pencil, but getting the code down to generate an image
